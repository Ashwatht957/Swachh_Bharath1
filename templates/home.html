Swachh Bharat Abhiyan

A step towards a cleaner and greener India through citizen participation.

What is Swachh Bharat Abhiyan?

Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Clean India Mission) is a national initiative launched by the Government of India on October 2, 2014, aiming to promote cleanliness, sanitation, and proper waste management across the country.

Objectives

Eliminate open defecation, improve waste management, and raise hygiene awareness.

Key Focus Areas
  • ✅ Construction of toilets
  • ✅ Waste management systems
  • ✅ Public awareness campaigns
Citizen Participation

Every citizen plays a vital role by keeping surroundings clean and spreading awareness.

How Citizens Can Participate

Your active involvement is essential for a cleaner India. Here’s how you can contribute:

1. Identify an Unclean Area

Observe and report places that need cleaning in your neighborhood, streets, or public areas.

2. Report the Location

Inform local authorities or use our portal to submit dirty location details and images.

3. Spread Awareness

Encourage friends, family, and neighbors to participate in cleanliness drives and events.
